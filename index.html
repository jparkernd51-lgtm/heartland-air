Who We Are

Built for Virtual Aviators

Heartland Air is a virtual airline dedicated to realistic, immersive flight simulation. Whether you fly narrow-body domestic routes or haul cargo overnight in a Caravan, there's a place for every pilot in our fleet.

Apply to Join
✈

Commercial Passenger Service

2,000+ daily scheduled routes from 8 hubs. Aircraft range from the A318 to the 787-9 Dreamliner, assigned by route distance.

📦

Dedicated Cargo Division

550+ overnight cargo flights daily. C172 and C182 for short hops, Caravans for regionals, Vision Jet for longer cargo legs.

📋

PIREP & Flight Logging

Log every flight, track hours by aircraft type, and build your career from regional piston ops to widebody international service.

🌐

VATSIM & IVAO Compatible

Full support for online ATC networks. Fly with real controllers on the Heartland Air callsign HL.
